## How to Apply

1. Click **Apply →** next to the role you want
2. Fill in the application form — your submission becomes a public profile
3. Share your application link with your network and ask them to react with 👍
4. The more support you collect, the higher you rank on the leaderboard and the sooner you get reviewed

Your application: `https://github.com/holdex/trial/issues/<your-issue-number>`
